How PrePeak Stabilizes Your Data Center

PrePeak combines real-time sensors, predictive AI algorithms, and intelligent energy buffers into one unified platform.

01

Real-Time Monitoring

Sensors continuously monitor current, voltage, and temperature across every rack and PDU in the data center.

02

AI Prediction Engine

Our AI learns power consumption patterns and predicts spikes milliseconds before they occur.

03

Instant Buffer Activation

Upon spike detection, the system instantly routes energy from dedicated intelligent buffers.

04

Full Stabilization

Infrastructure is stabilized before the load impacts any equipment — GPUs, UPS, or critical systems.
